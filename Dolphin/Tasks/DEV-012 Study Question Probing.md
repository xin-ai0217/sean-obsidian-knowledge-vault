# DEV-012 — Per-Question Probing Levels

**What:** Each question in a study can now have its own probing level: None (no follow-up), Light (one brief follow-up), or Deep (up to two focused follow-ups). Researcher sets this during study creation. Maya follows these per-question instructions during the interview.
